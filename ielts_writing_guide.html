Task 2 黃金架構（四段式）
✅ 題型一：同意 / 不同意（Agree or Disagree）
1

引言 Introduction（2～3 句）

改寫題目背景 → 表明立場（I agree / disagree with this view）

2

主體段落 1 — 主要論點

主題句 → 解釋 → 例子 → 結尾句連結主題

3

主體段落 2 — 次要論點 或 反駁

承認對立觀點 → 但用 "However" 反駁，強化立場

4

結論 Conclusion（2～3 句）

重申立場 → 簡短總結兩個論點，不加新資訊

⚖️ 題型二：討論兩面觀點（Discuss Both Views）
1

引言（2～3 句）

改寫題目 → 說明本文將討論兩方觀點並給出自己意見

2

觀點 A（3～4 句）

陳述一方觀點 → 解釋理由 → 舉例

3

觀點 B（3～4 句）

陳述另一方觀點 → 解釋理由 → 舉例

4

結論（給出個人意見）

總結兩方 → 說明自己更傾向哪一方及原因

Task 2 五大題型完整攻略
➕➖ 題型三：優缺點型（Advantages and Disadvantages）
題目特徵："Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?" 或 "Discuss the advantages and disadvantages."
1

引言（明確表明立場）

改寫題目背景 → 說明優點大於缺點 OR 缺點大於優點（or 兩者相當）

2

優點段落

主題句（優點）→ 解釋 → 舉例。建議寫 2 個優點，較支持的觀點放在字數較多的段落。

3

缺點段落

主題句（缺點）→ 解釋 → 舉例，但避免說「完全沒缺點」或「完全沒優點」。

4

結論（強調立場）

總結並重申整體是優點 > 缺點（或反之），呼應引言立場。

範例題目
The rise of online shopping has transformed the retail industry. Do the advantages of this development outweigh the disadvantages?
引言範本：The rapid growth of e-commerce has fundamentally changed the way people shop. While there are certain drawbacks to this trend, I believe that the benefits of online shopping significantly outweigh its disadvantages.
❓❓ 題型四：雙問題型（Two-Part / Direct Questions）
題目特徵：題目最後有兩個獨立問題，例如 "Why is this? What can be done about it?" ——兩個問題都必須回答！
1

引言

改寫背景 → 說明本文將回答兩個問題

2

回答第一個問題

完整回應問題一，需有解釋和例子（3～4 句）

3

回答第二個問題

完整回應問題二，需有解釋和例子（3～4 句）

4

結論

簡短總結兩個問題的答案

範例題目
In many countries, people are living longer than ever before. What are the reasons for this? Is this a positive or negative development?
⚠️ 最常見失分原因：只回答其中一個問題。即使答得很好，也會在 Task Response 項被嚴重扣分！

🔍 題型快速識別表

題目關鍵字題型立場要求
To what extent do you agree or disagree?同意/不同意型必須表明立場（可部分同意）
Discuss both views and give your own opinion.兩面觀點型必須討論兩邊 + 給意見
Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?優缺點型必須比較並表明哪邊更重
What are the causes/effects? What can be done?問題解決型描述問題 + 提解決方案
Why is this? Is this a positive/negative development?雙問題型兩個問題都必須完整回答

Task 2 範例題目與作答
範例題目（同意 / 不同意型）
Some people believe that technology has made our lives more complicated. To what extent do you agree or disagree?

✅ 高分範例作答（約 280 字）

It is often argued that modern technology has added unnecessary complexity to daily life. While I acknowledge that technology can be overwhelming at times, I firmly believe that its overall impact has been to simplify, rather than complicate, our lives.

To begin with, technology has dramatically streamlined many everyday tasks. Online banking, for instance, allows people to manage finances in minutes without visiting a branch. Similarly, navigation apps have eliminated the need to memorize routes or read physical maps. These conveniences save time and reduce cognitive load, making life objectively easier for the majority of people.

However, critics argue that keeping up with rapidly evolving devices — frequent software updates, cybersecurity concerns, and digital overload — creates its own form of stress. While this is a valid point, such challenges are typically temporary learning curves rather than permanent complications. Most people adapt quickly, and support systems such as tutorials and customer service help ease transitions.

In conclusion, although technology occasionally introduces short-term complexity, the long-term benefits of speed, convenience, and connectivity far outweigh these drawbacks. I therefore agree that, on balance, technology has simplified modern life rather than complicated it.

字數：約 213 字（實際考試建議寫 250～290 字）
範例題目（問題與解決方案型）
Many cities around the world are facing a growing problem of traffic congestion. What are the causes of this problem, and what measures can be taken to solve it?

✅ 架構示範（重點句型）

引言：Traffic congestion has become one of the most pressing urban challenges of the 21st century. This essay will examine the main causes of this issue and propose several effective solutions.

原因段：The primary cause is the rapid increase in private car ownership... Furthermore, inadequate public transport infrastructure forces people to rely on personal vehicles...

解決方案段：One effective measure would be to invest heavily in public transport networks... In addition, governments could implement congestion pricing schemes to discourage driving in city centres...

結論：In conclusion, traffic congestion stems from over-reliance on private cars and insufficient public transport. By improving infrastructure and introducing smart pricing policies, cities can significantly reduce gridlock.

Task 1 架構（圖表描述）
📊 圖表 / 數據型（三段式）
1

Overview 概述（最重要！）

用 1～2 句說明圖表最明顯的趨勢或特徵，不要看細節數字

2

主體段 1 — 主要趨勢

描述最顯著的數據變化，加入具體數字支撐

3

主體段 2 — 次要趨勢 / 比較

描述其他數據並做比較，結尾回扣 overview

Task 1 範例 Overview 句型
Overall, it is clear that [A] experienced the most significant growth over the period, while [B] showed a steady decline. The most notable trend is that [X] consistently outperformed all other categories throughout the years shown.
Task 1 範例題目（折線圖）
The graph below shows the percentage of people using the internet in three countries between 2000 and 2020. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.
📊 [假設圖表數據]：2000 年：Country A 20%、Country B 5%、Country C 2%
2020 年：Country A 85%、Country B 60%、Country C 35%

✅ Task 1 高分範例作答（約 175 字）

The line graph illustrates the proportion of the population in three countries who used the internet over a twenty-year period from 2000 to 2020.

Overall, internet usage rose considerably in all three countries over the period. Country A consistently had the highest rate of internet adoption, while Country C showed the most dramatic proportional increase despite starting from the lowest base.

In 2000, Country A led with approximately 20% of its population online, compared to just 5% in Country B and a mere 2% in Country C. By 2020, Country A's figures had climbed sharply to 85%, making it the clear frontrunner. Country B experienced steady growth, reaching 60% by the end of the period.

Country C, meanwhile, saw the most remarkable transformation — rising from just 2% to 35%, representing a 17-fold increase. Despite this, it remained the lowest of the three countries throughout the entire period shown.

字數：約 165 字 ✓ | 有 Overview ✓ | 有具體數字 ✓ | 有比較 ✓

📌 Task 1 不同圖型的寫法差異

圖型重點描述方向特殊注意事項
折線圖 Line Graph趨勢變化（上升/下降/波動）必須提到時間範圍
長條圖 Bar Chart數值比較（最高/最低/差距）注意是否有時間維度
圓餅圖 Pie Chart比例占比（最大/最小/相近）換算成百分比描述
表格 Table選最顯著的數據比較不要逐格描述，要篩選重點
流程圖 Process步驟順序（passive voice 為主）用 First / Then / Finally
地圖 Map位置變化（新增/移除/改建）用方位詞描述（to the north of...）
⚠️
中文考生最常犯的 8 大錯誤

以下是台灣/香港/大陸考生最容易因母語干擾而犯的錯誤，每個都是直接失分的地雷！

❶ 冠詞 a / an / the 亂用（或省略）
❌ 錯誤
Education is important part of life. Government should improve system.
✅ 正確
Education is an important part of life. The government should improve the system.
💡 規則：特指用 the，第一次提到用 a/an，不可數/抽象名詞通常不加冠詞
❷ 不可數名詞加 s
❌ 錯誤
People need more informations and knowledges about environments.
✅ 正確
People need more information and knowledge about the environment.
💡 不可數名詞：information, advice, knowledge, research, evidence, equipment, furniture, news
❸ 主詞動詞不一致
❌ 錯誤
The number of students who study abroad are increasing every year.
✅ 正確
The number of students who study abroad is increasing every year.
💡 "The number of + 複數名詞" → 動詞用單數。"A number of + 複數名詞" → 動詞用複數
❹ 中式英文直譯
❌ 中式英文
"People's living standard is more and more high." / "The economy has a very big development."
✅ 自然英文
"Living standards are rising significantly." / "The economy has developed substantially."
💡 避免把中文句子逐字翻譯。遇到不確定的表達，換一種更簡單但地道的方式說。
❺ 過度使用 "and" / "also" 作連接詞
❌ 錯誤
Technology is useful. And it saves time. Also it is convenient. And people use it every day.
✅ 正確
Technology is useful because it not only saves time but also offers convenience, making it an indispensable part of modern life.
❻ 時態混亂
❌ 錯誤
In the past, people travel by horse. Nowadays technology changed how we move.
✅ 正確
In the past, people travelled by horse. Nowadays, technology has changed how we move.
💡 議論文整體用現在式，描述過去用過去式，強調「已經」的變化用現在完成式
❼ 引言直接抄題目
❌ 錯誤（照抄）
題目："Technology has made our lives more complicated." 引言也寫："Technology has made our lives more complicated."
✅ 正確（改寫）
"Modern advancements have arguably added layers of complexity to everyday existence."（同義替換：technology→modern advancements, complicated→complexity）
❽ 論點空泛，沒有具體例子
❌ 空泛
Technology is good for education. Students can learn many things. It is very helpful for them.
✅ 具體
Technology enhances education significantly. Platforms such as Khan Academy allow students to access high-quality lessons at no cost, enabling self-paced learning that traditional classrooms cannot always provide.
💡 公式：主題句 → 解釋原因 → 具體例子（人名/地名/機構名/數據）→ 回扣主題

Band 6.0 vs Band 7.0：差在哪裡？
6.0

這個分數的作文通常是…

  • 回應了題目，但不夠完整或部分偏題
  • 段落有組織，但連接詞使用較機械化（"Firstly... Secondly... Finally..."）
  • 詞彙重複，較少使用同義詞替換
  • 文法有一些錯誤，尤其是冠詞和時態
  • 句型以簡單句為主，缺少複合句或複雜句
  • 結論只是重複引言，沒有新的提升
7.0

這個分數的作文通常是…

  • 完整清晰地回應所有題目要求
  • 段落邏輯流暢，連接詞使用自然多樣
  • 詞彙豐富，會根據語境選擇精準的詞彙
  • 文法偶爾有小錯誤，但不影響理解
  • 靈活使用多種句型（關係子句、條件句、被動式等）
  • 每個主體段有清晰的主題句 + 發展 + 例子

✍️ 同樣論點，6分 vs 7分的寫法對比

論點：科技幫助教育
Band 6.0 寫法
Technology is very helpful for education. Students can use the internet to study. They can find many things online. This is good for learning.
→ 詞彙重複(good/helpful)、無具體例子、句型單一
Band 7.0 寫法
Technology has revolutionised the way students learn. Digital platforms such as Coursera and Khan Academy provide free access to world-class courses, enabling learners in remote areas to acquire skills that were previously unavailable to them. This democratisation of education is arguably one of the most significant benefits of the digital age.
→ 具體例子、豐富詞彙、複合句型

實戰練習題（按題型分類）

以下是各題型的高頻練習題，建議計時練習（Task 2 限時 40 分鐘，Task 1 限時 20 分鐘）。

同意/不同意型
Some people argue that the best way to reduce crime is to give longer prison sentences. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this opinion?
💡 提示：思考其他減少犯罪的方式（教育、社會支持、貧困根源）→ 反駁「長期監禁萬能論」
兩面觀點型
Some people believe that children should be taught to compete. Others believe that cooperation is more important. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.
💡 提示：競爭的優點（進步動力）vs 合作的優點（社會和諧）→ 最後表明自己的立場
優缺點型
Many people are now working from home rather than going to an office. Do the advantages of this trend outweigh the disadvantages?
💡 提示：優點（彈性、省通勤時間）vs 缺點（孤立感、工作生活難以區分）→ 明確表明哪方更重
問題解決型
Air pollution has become a serious problem in many cities worldwide. What are the main causes of this problem? What measures could be taken to address it?
💡 提示：原因（工業排放、車輛尾氣）→ 解決方案（可再生能源、大眾運輸）
雙問題型
In many countries, the number of young people choosing to study abroad is increasing. Why do you think this is? Is this a positive or negative development?
💡 注意：這題有兩個問題！第一段回答「為什麼」，第二段回答「是否正面/負面」，兩個都要完整作答！
Task 1 練習
The chart below shows the percentage of household income spent on various categories in the UK in 1985 and 2015. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.
💡 提示：先找最大/最小的類別 → 比較 1985 和 2015 的變化 → Overview 說明整體趨勢。記得寫 150 字以上！

關鍵答題技巧
🚫

絕對不要改寫題目照抄

引言要用同義字替換改寫題目，直接複製會被扣分（TA / TR 項）。

📏

字數要達標

Task 1 不少於 150 字，Task 2 不少於 250 字。字數不足會直接被扣分。

🧩

每段只有一個中心思想

每個主體段落開頭寫主題句，之後的句子都要支撐該主題句。

🔀

句型要多樣化

混合使用簡單句、複合句、條件句（If...）、關係子句（which / that...）。

⏰

先做 Task 2

Task 2 佔分較多，建議先完成。Task 1 放最後。考前分配：Task 2（40 分鐘）、Task 1（20 分鐘）。

🔁

留 2～3 分鐘檢查

快速掃描拼寫錯誤、主詞動詞一致性、冠詞 a/an/the 的使用是否正確。

📝

Task 1：一定要寫 Overview

Overview 是 Task 1 最重要的段落。沒有 Overview 幾乎不可能拿到 6 分以上。

🎯

避免重複使用同一個詞

例如 "good" 可改為 beneficial / advantageous / positive；"show" 可改為 illustrate / indicate / demonstrate。

🚀
短時間衝刺計畫

⚡ 考前倒數 — 每日任務

Day 1～2
熟背四段式架構 + 連接詞清單。各練寫一篇 Task 2 引言和結論（不用全篇）。
Day 3～4
完整練寫一篇 Task 2（限時 40 分鐘）。重點：確保字數達 250+ 且每段有主題句。
Day 5
練寫一篇 Task 1 圖表描述（限時 20 分鐘）。確保有 Overview 段落，使用數字和圖表詞彙。
考試前夜
複習連接詞清單 + 高頻替換詞彙。不要練寫全篇文章，保存體力。

💡 最高 CP 值的備考重點（時間有限時）

  1. 背熟引言改寫公式（同義字替換題目）
  2. 練到可以在 5 分鐘內寫好Task 2 引言 + 結論
  3. 記住 Task 1 必須有 Overview，且 Overview 放在第二段（引言之後）
  4. 學會 10 個以上的圖表動詞（rise, fall, plateau, fluctuate...）
  5. 每個主體段落都要有具體例子或數據，不能只有空泛論述
